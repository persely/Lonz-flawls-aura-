Our Story

Lonz Flawls Aura was born in the Cape—a fusion of science, soul, and South African radiance. Our founder, a passionate skin scientist, believed that beauty is both heritage and innovation.

With every product, we weave tradition and cutting-edge research into a ritual of self-love. Aura is for every shade, flaw, and story—because true glow is never filtered.

Our Ethics

Our Science

Every Aura formula is the result of years of research, field testing, and dermatologist review. We use native South African botanicals—marula, rooibos, baobab—combined with next-gen actives for visible, lasting results.

  • Clinically proven, never exaggerated
  • Peer-reviewed studies for every claim
  • No parabens, phthalates, or mineral oil
  • Non-comedogenic, inclusive for all skin types
